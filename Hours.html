Hours and direction of the bridge

The Golden Gate Bridge is located on US Highway 101, providing a vital link between the City and County of San Francisco and the counties to the north. The Bridge does not have a physical address but it can be easily found on Google Maps or other online map services. The bridge is open daily. The Bridge Pavilion is open 9 a.m. to 6 p.m.
